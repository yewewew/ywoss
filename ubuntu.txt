Ubuntu is a free and open-sourcd Linux distribution based on Debian.
Ubuntu is officially released in three editions: Desktop, Server, and Core(for internet of things devices and robots). Ubuntu is a popular operating system for cloud computing, with support for OpenStack.
Ubuntu is released every six months, with long-term support releases every two years. The latest release is 19.04, and the most recent long-term support release is 18.04 LTS, which is supported until 2028..
